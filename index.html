About Us

Munshi Singh College M.S. College was founded in the year 1945 in the district head quarter of East Champaran, Motihari with a gigantic vision to contribute in the betterment of educational scenario of North Bihar. It was made a constituent unit of Babasaheb Bhimrao Ambedkar Bihar University Muzaffarpur in 1980 and included in the list of colleges maintained under section 2(f) and 12(B) of the UGC Act, 1956 under the head Government College.
