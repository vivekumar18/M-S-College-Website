Facilities

MS College Motihari Campus, Facilities & Infrastructure MS College Motihari campus is located in Motihari, Bihar. MS College campus is home to all the administrative buildings, college classrooms, and extracurricular infrastructure. Students can also access various other facilities at MS College Motihari, like banking services, gyms, medical facilities and more.

World Class Library

The College library is automated using Integrated Library Management System (ILMS). The Library has subscribed to e-granthalaya software for automation of library services. The students and teachers are given training in using e-granthalaya software for quick access of books and references required by students and teachers.

Largest playground

Our College has an excellent play ground for students with indoor and out door sports facilities. Our college is having very big playground for various outdoor games. Various sports are conducted by coachers

Auditorium

A well furnished auditorium which can accommodate more than 300 students. The auditorium is routinely used for guest lectures, technical seminars, pre-placement training and extra-curricular activities.

About Us

Munshi Singh College M.S. College was founded in the year 1945 in the district head quarter of East Champaran, Motihari with a gigantic vision
to contribute in the betterment of educational scenario of North Bihar.
